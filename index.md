# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 6, 2026

## Tsurerukana? Fishing Japan

Tsurerukana? Fishing Japan ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") provides fishing condition forecasts, fish species recommendations, and lure recommendations for selected lakes in Japan.
This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

## Information We Collect

The App is designed to minimize data collection.
We do not collect or request personal information such as:

* Name
* Email address
* Phone number
* Home or mailing address
* Payment information
* Contacts
* Photos or media files

The App does not require user registration or account creation.

## Location Information

The App does not access, collect, or use your device's GPS location.
All fishing locations and lake coordinates used by the App are predefined and built into the application.

## Weather Data

The App obtains weather and environmental information from publicly available weather data providers through third-party weather APIs.
This information is used solely to generate fishing forecasts, fishing condition scores, fish activity estimates, and lure recommendations.
Weather requests are not used to identify individual users.
Please note that weather API providers may process technical information such as IP addresses as part of standard internet communications. Such processing is governed by the privacy policies of the respective service providers.

## Fishing Forecast Calculations

The App generates fishing forecasts and recommendations using proprietary calculations and algorithms.
These calculations may incorporate:

* Weather conditions
* Atmospheric pressure
* Wind conditions
* Seasonal factors
* Lake-specific information

No personal information is required to perform these calculations.

## Crash Reporting and Diagnostics

To improve application stability and reliability, the App uses Firebase Crashlytics, a crash reporting service provided by Google.
When the App experiences an unexpected error or crash, Firebase Crashlytics may automatically collect technical diagnostic information, including:

* Device model
* Device operating system version
* App version
* Crash logs
* Stack traces
* General diagnostic information related to application failures

This information is used solely to identify, investigate, and resolve software issues and improve the performance and reliability of the App.
Crash reports are not used to personally identify users.

## Analytics and Tracking

The App does not use advertising services.
The App does not track users across third-party apps or websites.
The App does not use advertising identifiers for marketing or behavioral advertising purposes.
Firebase Crashlytics is used exclusively for crash reporting and diagnostics.

## Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services that process limited technical information as part of their operation, including:

* Weather data providers
* Firebase Crashlytics (Google)

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

Google Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Firebase Crashlytics:
https://firebase.google.com/products/crashlytics

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information.
Because the App does not collect personal information, there is generally no personal data to share with third parties.
Technical diagnostic information collected through Firebase Crashlytics is processed by Google for the purpose of providing crash reporting services.

## Data Retention

The App does not maintain user accounts and does not store personal information on our servers.
Crash reports collected through Firebase Crashlytics are retained according to Google's applicable retention policies.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed toward children under the age of 13.
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so that appropriate action can be taken.

## Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the services used by the App and to maintain the security of collected technical information.
However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last updated" date will be revised accordingly.
Your continued use of the App after any changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
